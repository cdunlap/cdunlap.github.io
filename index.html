I'm a syntactically diverse software developer living in the Los Angeles, CA area of the United States. I hold an Associate's degree in Computer Information Systems, a Bachelor's degree in Game and Simulation Programming, and I've been writing software personally and professionally since 1999.

The programming languages in which I am experienced include C, C++, C#, PHP, and JavaScript--just to name a few. I have experience in creating mobile, embedded, desktop, command-line, web, and video game applications for consumer, business, and government/defense purposes.

I invite you to look through my portfolio. Most of what you find here will be my video game and web projects. If you see something you like, shoot me an email, or reach out on Twitter, LinkedIn, or any of the other social networks on which you can easily find me.
